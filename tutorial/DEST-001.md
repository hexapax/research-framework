# DEST-001: Outer Banks -- Kill Devil Hills, NC

## Summary

The Outer Banks is a string of barrier islands off the coast of North Carolina, known for wide beaches, historic sites, and a laid-back beach town atmosphere. It is one of the most popular East Coast beach destinations, offering a mix of nature, history, and water sports without the commercial density of places like Myrtle Beach.

## What to Do

- **Beaches**: Miles of public beach access. Less crowded than Virginia Beach, especially south of Nags Head.
- **Wright Brothers Memorial**: Walk the field where powered flight began. Small museum on site.
- **Jockey's Ridge**: Hike the sand dunes, hang glide, or watch the sunset from the top.
- **Corolla Wild Horses**: Take a 4x4 tour to see the wild mustangs in the northern OBX.
- **Cape Hatteras Lighthouse**: Climb the tallest brick lighthouse in the US (257 steps).
- **Dining**: Try the crab cakes at Awful Arthur's or the fish tacos at Tortugas' Lie.

## Known Issues

- Seasonal closures are not mentioned in the Expedia listing, which could mislead winter travelers.
- Rental pricing varies wildly by week and property size; the per-person estimate is a rough average.
