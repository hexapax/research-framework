# DEST-005: Wintergreen Resort -- Roseland, VA

## Summary

Wintergreen Resort is a four-season mountain resort on the eastern slope of the Blue Ridge Mountains in Nelson County, Virginia. It offers skiing in winter and golf, hiking, and swimming in summer. The resort is close to Richmond but reviews are mixed, with lodging quality varying significantly between individually-owned condo units.

## What to Do

- **Winter**: Ski or snowboard on 26 trails with 1,003 feet of vertical drop. Not large by western standards, but the closest real skiing to Richmond.
- **Summer**: Two golf courses (Devil's Knob at the summit, Stoney Creek at the base), outdoor pool, tennis, and mountain biking.
- **Nature**: Shamokin Springs nature preserve has easy trails through old-growth forest. The Plunge waterfall hike is 3.4 miles round trip.
- **Nelson County**: The "Trail" of craft breweries, cideries, and wineries (Bold Rock, Blue Mountain Brewery, Veritas Vineyard) is a major draw even without staying at the resort.
- **Spa**: Indoor pool and spa facilities. (**NOTE: Spa closed for renovation as of January 2026 -- check status before booking.**)

## Known Issues

- **Rating gap**: TripAdvisor (3.5) and Google (4.0) disagree by half a point. The TripAdvisor reviews focus on lodging quality and resort fees; Google reviews focus on the ski mountain and outdoor activities. Both are valid but they describe different experiences.
- **Outdated cost data**: The $110/night estimate is from April 2025. The resort raised management fees in late 2025. Current rates should be checked before planning.
- **Spa closure**: The spa has been closed for renovation since January 2026 with no announced reopening date. At least one travel blog still lists it as available, which is incorrect.
- **Condo lottery**: Because units are individually owned, booking through the resort means you may get a recently renovated unit or one that has not been updated since the 1990s. Reading recent reviews for specific building numbers helps.
